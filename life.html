About My Photography

Photography has been my passion since my undergraduate years. It serves as a creative outlet that complements my scientific research. Through my lens, I capture the beauty of nature, architecture, and everyday moments. Each photo tells a story and reflects my perspective on the world around me. I believe that both science and art are essential for understanding and appreciating the complexity of our universe.
